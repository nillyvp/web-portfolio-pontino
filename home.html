Navigating life with purpose through data science for good.

Hi, I’m Nilly Pontino – a self-taught coder, ex-finance professional, and data analyst, currently learning about machine learning. “Nile” had been one of the nicknames bestowed to me in the past; hence my website, Nile River.

On to the “river” part. A river bears witness to the rise and fall of human civilizations. It ceaselessly flows into the ocean and forms a crucial part of the ecological system. Rivers remind me how truly interconnected we are. In “What is a River?”, Monika Vaicenavičienė writes –

“A river is a thread. It embroiders our world with beautiful patterns. It connects people and places, past and present. It stitches stories together.“

I feel the same with data science. When used for good, data science weaves a tapestry of stories and patterns that we can continuously learn from – for the advancement of humanity.
